Badminton

When I was younger me and my sister would always play badminton. It's another acitivity I like to do when I have free time because it's great for exercise and helps clear up my mind.

  • Experience: Amature
  • Difficulty: Medium to Hard
  • Duration: 40-50 minutes
  • Match: Singles and Doubles

Badminton is a fast pace sport and you could play singles or doubles. You would score a point if your opponent fails to serve the birdie back to you or if they hit out of bounds. Additionally, when your birdie hits the net your opponent would get the point. back and forth. I like to play badminton because it's back and forth.
