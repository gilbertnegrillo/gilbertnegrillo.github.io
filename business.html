Purpose

I want to create a business portfolio because it is one the key characteristic in lading a web developer job. Having a business portfolio will most likely increase my chances of landing my first job as a web developer.

Planning

My business portfolio will need to have 3 to 4 quality projects to meet the expectations of a company. I will need to match what the business needs and showcase to them what I am capable of. These business projects need to practical for the company to understand.

Features

The most important feature of my business portfolio is going to be the stack software. A company will use a specific stack and I will have to prove to them that I am capable of working with that stack. The overall business portfolio will need to look beautiful.
