Purpose

Fashion and clothing are two of my favorite things and I like to wear streetwear, casual, and professional. Finding the right fit can be hard and I have experienced that and I want to build a website that will give people fasion ideas and inspiration.

Planning

This website will consist of having the latest fashion trends for everyone to see. People can browse at all the different clothing style's and the models on the website will be people from all over the world. People can also share their own fashion style for others to see.

Features

There will be an two different options people can browse, affordable and high end. Those that are looking for alternative and low prices on clothing can visit the afforadble page. People can click on each clothing piece on the model that takes them to the actual website of that brand if they want to buy it.
