Purpose

A web app that I've considered creating is to improve workflow. I want to create this web app because at times, I find myself going on my phone when I'm supposed to be doing my assignments. The purpose of this is to help you get your work done on time, get better time management skills, and prevent any distractions.

Planning

All you have to do is set a timer on how long you're going to work for. Once you've finished setting your timer, it will turn off your notifications for you. When you're supposed to be working and you go on your phone, the app will recognize it and more time will be added to your timer, rather than locking you out of your own phone. If needed, you can also add more time.

Features

It will include a feature that will tell you how much longer you have left on your timer. One thing to take in consideration is you can pause the timer when you decide to take a break. After the timer is finished, it will give you a summary on how much time was added to your timer, how long you took a break, and the number of times you used your phone.
